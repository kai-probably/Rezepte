# Aquafaba Mousse au Chocolat

## Ingredients

Water from 250g can of chickpeas

140g of zartbitter chocolate

## Instructions

1. beat chickpeas water to a foam (8-10min)
2. melt chocolate in a waterbath (a bowl sitting on top of a pot with boiling water)
3. gently add chocolate to foam with a spatula
4. chill at least 3 hours
