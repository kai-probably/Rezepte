## Tan Tan Don Kai

## Ingredients for 3-4 servings

* &#x20;460g ground turkey (or other minced meat)
* &#x20;3 cloves garlic, chopped
* &#x20;oil
* &#x20;garlic salt
* &#x20;black pepper, ground
* &#x20;340g french green beans chopped
* &#x20;2 cups/ 200 g of rice
* &#x20;4 tablespoons (60ml) hoisin sauce
* &#x20;1½ tablespoons (15ml) chilli bean sauce
* &#x20;1½ tablespoons (15ml) sriracha sauce
* &#x20;1 tablespoon sesame oil
* &#x20;bunch cilantro chopped (coriander)
* &#x20;2 stalks green onions chopped
* &#x20;Japanese mayonnaise

## Short recipe

<br>

1. Sauté 460g of minced **meat** in **oil** with the **3 chopped garlic cloves**
2. Add **garlic** **salt and pepper**
3. Sauté until brown
4. Add 340g chopped french **green beans**
5. Cook for about 10-15min
6. (Time to start the rice (2 cups))
7. Add **60ml hoisin sauce, 15ml chilli bean sauces, 15ml sriracha sauce** and **1 tablespoon sesame oil** and mix well
8. Add over the finished rice
9. Top with **chopped green onions,** **cilantro** (coriander) and mayonnaise
10. Serve

### Serves 2

Soup

4 cups of chicken stock

2 tablespoons chili bean sauce

2 tablespoons sesame paste

1 tablespoon soy sauce

1 tablespoon sugar

salt and pepper to taste

Meat

1/2 lb ground pork

1 tablespoon minced garlic

1 tablespoon minced shallots

1 sliced thai red chili

1 tablespoon soy sauce

1 teaspoon vegetable oil

1 teaspoon sesame oil

salt and pepper to taste

noodles of your favourite variety (I used ramen)

sliced green onions for garnish

Heat up the 4 cups of **chicken stock** and stir in the 2 tbsp. **chili bean sauce**, 2 tbsp. **sesame paste,** 1 tbsp. **soy sauce**, and 1 tbsp. **sugar**. Keep the soup on a low simmer while you cook the **meat**.

In a small wok or frying pan, heat up the **teaspoon of vegetable oil** over medium high heat and add the **minced garlic** and **shallots**. Fry until fragrant, but not browned. Add the **ground pork** and cook until the pork is browned and there is no pink. Add the **sliced chili**, 1 tbsp. **soy sauce**, and 1 tbsp. **sesame oil**. Remove from the heat and keep warm while you cook the noodles.

Cook the noodles according to the packaging and drain. Place the noodles in a bowl and top with broth and ground pork. Garnish with green onions and enjoy!
