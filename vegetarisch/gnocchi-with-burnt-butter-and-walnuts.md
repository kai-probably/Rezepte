# Gnocchi with burnt butter and walnuts

**7 Ingredients**

* 1 quantity Basic Potato Gnocchi (see recipe on taste.com.au)
* 125g butter, chopped
* ~~1/2 cup sage leaves~~
* 2 garlic cloves, crushed
* 120g pkt Coles Australian Baby Spinach
* 1/2 cup (50g) walnuts, toasted, chopped
* Grated parmesan, to serve

**4 Method Steps**

* 1\. Line a large baking tray with baking paper. Bring a large saucepan of water to the boil. Add one-quarter of **gnocchi** (1 quantity Basic Potato Gnocchi). Cook for 2-3 mins or until the gnocchi rise to surface of the pan. Use a slotted spoon to transfer to lined tray. Repeat, in 3 batches, with remaining gnocchi.
* 2\. Meanwhile, melt the **butter** (125g butter, chopped) in a large frying pan over medium heat. Cook for 2-3 mins or until the butter melts and begins to foam. Add the **sage** (1/2 cup sage leaves) and cook for 1-2 mins or until crisp. Use a slotted spoon to transfer the sage to a plate.
* 3\. Add the **garlic** (2 garlic cloves, crushed), **gnocchi** and **spinach** (120g pkt Coles Australian Baby Spinach) to the pan. Season. Cook, tossing, until the gnocchi is coated in butter mixture and the spinach wilts.
* 4\. Divide the gnocchi mixture among serving bowls. Sprinkle with **fried sage** and **walnuts** (1/2 cup / 50g walnuts, toasted, chopped). Sprinkle with parmesan (Grated parmesan, to serve).
