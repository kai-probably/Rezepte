# Broccoli & Pak Choi Soba

## 2 Servings

* [ ] ⅓ broccoli&#x20;
* [ ] 200 g bok choy&#x20;
* [ ] ½ lemon&#x20;
* [ ] 10 g sesame seeds&#x20;
* [ ] 120 g soba noodles&#x20;
* [ ] 2 tsp sesame oil, toasted&#x20;
* [ ] 1 tbsp rice vinegar&#x20;
* [ ] 1 tsp Thai chili sauce (Sriracha)&#x20;
* [ ] 2 tbsp peanut oil&#x20;
* [ ] 2 clove(s) garlic&#x20;
* [ ] 1 tbsp maple syrup&#x20;
* [ ] 2 tbsp soy sauce&#x20;
* [ ] ¼ tsp chili flake

## Cook

1. Peel and finely slice garlic.
2. Wash bok choy, dry, and slice off stalks. Halve large leaves lengthwise.
3. Wash broccoli and cut into florets.
4. Heat peanut oil in a large pan and fry broccoli, bok choi, and garlic on medium heat until vegetables are tender and begin to brown.
5. Cook noodles according to the package instructions. Once cooked, plunge into ice water to prevent them from sticking together.
6. Halve and juice lemon.
7. Whisk together lemon juice, sriracha, sesame oil, vinegar, soy sauce, maple syrup, and chili flakes.
8. Toss noodles with dressing and vegetables. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and enjoy.
