# Avocado Pasta Salad

## Ingredients

### For the Salad

* 230 g whole-grain pasta
* 200 g cherry tomatoes
* 1 green onion
* 1 avocado
* 200 g low-fat feta
* 2 Tbsp cilantro

### For the Dressing

* 1 avocado
* 100 ml buttermilk
* juice of ½ lime
* ½ tsp garlic powder
* salt & pepper

## Instructions

1. Cook 230g **pasta** according to the package directions.
2. In the meantime, dice 200g **cherry tomatoes**, one **green onion**, one **avocado** and 200g **feta** and chop the **cilantro**.
3. For the dressing, blend the flesh of the **avocado**, **100ml of buttermilk**, the **juice of ½ lime** and the **spices** until the dressing has a creamy consistency.
4. Then combine the pasta with the other ingredients and the dressing, and **refrigerate** the salad for at least **one hour** before serving.

{% hint style="info" %}
If you are vegan, just leave out the buttermilk and feta. As a source of protein, you could use tofu instead. Plus, a dash of starchy pasta water makes the dressing extra creamy.One serving of pasta salad contains about 420 calories, 52 g of carbohydrates, 25 g of protein and 17 g of fat.Try; Ricotta instead of Feta.
{% endhint %}
